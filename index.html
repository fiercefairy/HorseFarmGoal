Daily Schedule for horse and you!

  • Feed horses hay and/or grain morning and night.
  • Clean and refill water buckets morning and night.
  • Muck out stalls morning and night.
  • Replace fresh bedding if you have stalls and not 24 / 7 turnout.
  • Spray horse with fly spray or insect repellant morning and evening during summer months.
  • Ride your horse at least 3 days a week!
  • Wash saddle pads weekly.
  • Teach your horse to be patient when being turned loose.
  • Clean out stall and pasture twice a day.
    • Do daily horse stretches before you put on the saddle.

    • Tips!

  • Use olive oil to get rid of scratches on your saddle!
  • Use baby diapers to hold ointment and put on enjerys
  • Have thrush ointment - horse hoof bacteria is bad!
  • Get cowboy magic detangler!
  • Get horse rubber bands
  • Apple sider vinegar works on horse warts (somewhat...)
  • Keep horses away from manure so flys dont bite them.
  • In the summer time make sure you let your horse stand in a puddle on a tarp for a few minutes to help their hoofs. Only do this if its super dry where you are.
